The Best Movies According to Sharon

My top 3 movies of all-time

Highway

This is an drama about a young woman who gets accidently kidnapped. As a child abuse survivor she finds freedom in company of her captor.

Rockstar

One of my most favourite movies. It has fantastic music. It is a story about an amatuer musician who finds his inner voice after experiencing a breakup and is kicked out of his home and experiences homelessness.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

I can watch this movie any number of times. Its a light hearted movie about 3 young men on a bachelor trip to Spain. Every frame of this movie is so beautiful
